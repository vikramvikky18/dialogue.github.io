Arvinda Sametha

meeru evaro.. endho..naaku telidhu saamy.. , kanukkunta pedda kastamemi kaadu.
meeru ethhukochhina ammi naku kavalsini manishi
e poddu meeru aameni emaina cheyochhu, kaani repu..naadi..
ippati daanka meeru oka magadu aadadanni cherechedi vinuntaru
kaani ippudu meeku oka magadu magadini cheriste etta untado chupistanu
ee yala nunchi
ae palle lo ae komma lo aaku alladina
A kottam lo aavu arachina
A chettu meda pitta lechina
Adi mahanandi gari vera raghava reddy kathi vadhana alikide
gurthupettukondi meelo prathi vokkadu nannu champamani na kallu patti vedey varaku himsinchi champutha..
e kshanam nunchi mimmalni bhaya pettedi chaavu, mimmalni ventadedhi
chaavu,chivariki meeru korukunedhi kuda chhaaveyyy..

saavu kanuka gonthu unte itne untaada..

balreddy... gonthu gurthupattinava..
nee pakkanaunna pillodni podichipothaanantey poo..
nuvvu ooru cherey lopu tarumukuntaa vachhi ninnu narikesthaa
naaku eydnunchi banglore daggara,mundu akkadiki pothaa..
akkada chaduvuthunna nee kodukuni champestha..
palle cherey lopu aadu poyina vaartha vindhuu..
neeku poorthiga edchey time kuda.. ivvanu balreddy..
nalla mabbu aakasanni kamminattu nalla gudini kammestha.. ledu..
e ranam antha enduku anukunte aa pillodni aadney dimpesi po..
neeku santhi.. ela untundo chupistaa nuvvu uhinchavu antha adbuthanga chupistaa
